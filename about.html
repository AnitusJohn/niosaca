About NIOS

The National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS) was founded by the Ministry of Education, Government of India, to provide education to students who cannot attend regular schools.

How to Download NIOS Hall Ticket

BOSSE – Board of Open Schooling & Skill Education

BOSSE is a Government of Sikkim recognized open schooling board approved by NIOS, COBSE, AIU, UGC, Indian Army and more.

BOSSE Admission 2026 – Eligibility

  • Class 10: Minimum 14 Years
  • Class 12: Passed Class 10 from any recognized board

BOSSE On-Demand Exam (ODE) 2026

BOSSE ODE allows students to appear for exams anytime when they are ready.
  • Exam Window Opens: April 2026
  • Last Date: 10 May 2026
  • Result: 45 – 60 Days

Why Choose BOSSE ODE?

  • Fast Track Results
  • Flexible Exam Dates
  • Valid for NEET, JEE, UPSC
  • Accepted for Government & Private Jobs

Frequently Asked Questions

Is BOSSE valid?
Yes. BOSSE is Government approved and valid in India & abroad.

Is BOSSE valid for NEET?
Yes, BOSSE certificates are valid for NEET, JEE & UPSC.

How fast is BOSSE ODE result?
Usually declared within 45–60 days.
